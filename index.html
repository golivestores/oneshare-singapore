About ONE SHARE

Enterprise value does not become visible on its own.
It must be clearly expressed and carefully structured.

Based in Singapore, ONE SHARE provides integrated capital markets services across corporate expression, structural planning, and strategic execution. From investor relations and equity incentives to cross-border listings and M&A, we support companies through critical stages of their capital journey. These functions may appear separate, but together they shape how a company is understood, evaluated, and positioned in the market.

Building a Capital Visibility Architecture

After working closely with companies through different stages of their capital journey, we gradually arrived at one conviction—

A company's position in the capital markets is not determined by any single relationship. Serving issuers alone is not enough to fully understand the market; looking only from the investor side also fails to reveal how a company truly operates internally.

How a company is understood, evaluated, and positioned often depends on whether the relationships among issuers, investors, and employees are considered together.

That is why ONE SHARE works across these three key relationships, transforming them into a cohesive, integrated service framework.
